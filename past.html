Some history about me.

I officially began my coding journey in 2023. As a first-year senior high student, I was nervous because I had ZERO coding experience. But, for a long time I wanted to be in an industry that passionately keeps creating. Therefore, combining grit and my love for games, I took the first step and picked my year 11 subjects.


I picked Software Design and Development despite having no idea what the keyboard shortcuts are or even thinking Microsoft Edge was a decent web browser. It was challenging at first, but eventually I managed to understand the content. I am confident in Python, and I am very eager to learn more coding languages and master them.
