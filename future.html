What are my plans now?


In the future, I plan to take on commissions as an artist. This can range from making concept art for characters or scenes, 3D modelling environments or characters, or creating sprite sheets for pixel games.


I also plan to focus on my personal projects while exploring new opportunities. Kind of scared of what the future has in store for me, but I am very excited to share my stories and games to many people around the world.



If you want to follow my journey, you can follow me on Instagram!
